About

Discover Smart Metrics and our Vision360° project.

  • 2025

    Creation of Smart Metrics

    Smart Metrics was founded in 2025 with the ambition to turn complex data into clear and actionable insights, specializing in Business Intelligence and financial analysis.

  • Mid-2025

    Birth of Vision360°

    Vision360° was launched to provide a complete and real-time view of financial and operational KPIs, empowering decision-makers with intuitive dashboards and strategic metrics.

  • 2026

    Expanding Our Expertise

    Building on the success of Vision360°, Smart Metrics expanded its services to include anomaly detection, supplier trust analysis, financial KPIs optimization, and customer satisfaction analytics.

  • Today

    Our Commitment

    Today, Smart Metrics continues to innovate, delivering precision, speed, and clarity to help organizations unlock the full potential of their data.
